Survey Codifier Metrics Question Types
# Metric Description
1 Relevance Gauges how relevant the asset is to the user, seeing whether or not it is relevant to their demographic. Ex How relevant is the ad to your choice of Superbowl ads?
2 Lift Takes into account both the pre and post survey to see if the asset had an impact on the user. Ex. How much interested are you in seeing the ad of XXXXXXXX in Superbowl? Ex. Based on the preview you just saw, how much interested are you in seeing the ads of XXXXXXXX in the Superbowl?
3 Virality Attempts to display how likely it would be for the user to share this content and finds it relatable Ex. How would you characterize this advertisement? Please select all that apply.
4 Persuasion Did the asset effectively persuade the user into pursuing the marketed product. Ex. How likely are you to take the following actions?
5 Intent This metric works in tandem with Lift, gauging the intent of the user in pursuing the marketed product. Ex. Based on the preview you just saw, how much interested are you in seeing the ads of XXXXXXXX in the Superbowl?
6 Recall Identifies if the user was able to successfully remeber the creator of the content of they had just watched. Ex. Who was the sponsor of the video you just saw?
7 Awareness Tests the users knowledge of the brand or content prior to viewing the asset. Ex. Which of the following companyâ€™s ad have you watch in the Superbowl? Please select all that apply.
